Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information accessed when you use "saveme Call Timer" app. It also has information about why and how it stored and used.
When you use app "saveme Call Timer", you give it certain information voluntarily.

saveme uses information about:

  • ● contacts list
  • ● storage

saveme can't give or share this information to anyone but back to you. Because of obvious technical reasons.

saveme uses permissions:

  • ● make a phone call

saveme is a call timer and it has to make a call.

Contacts List

Contacts list used by you and only for picking a number which is needed to make a phone call possible.
You can write the number manually or pick it from the contact list. The choise is yours.
No any data collected, but choosed contacts for making a phone call after timer ended and not stopped.
They are stored inside of flile "numbers_list.json".

Storage Access

Storage access needed to save and load two files: "numbers_list.json" for list of choosed numbers and "timer_setting.json" for current timer configuration.
Storage access used for the reason of NOT USING internet connection and because of faster work in a critical situation.
Application works WITHOUT INTERNET, it cannot give your information to anyone.
For a try to give any information to any person it will need the internet connection and it has NO internet connection.

Access to a phone call

Application uses "dangerous" permission which is called "ACTION_CALL", to actually make a phone call. It is a purpose of app.
It is even have a name for it which is "saveme Call Timer".
